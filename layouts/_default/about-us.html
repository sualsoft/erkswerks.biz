Empowering Visionary Leadership with Eric Boisjoli

Welcome to Erk's Werks Business Group, where we believe in the power of visionary leadership and the transformative potential of strategic acquisitions. Our journey is led by the accomplished entrepreneur, Eric Boisjoli, a driving force in the world of business and a trailblazer in the art of creating thriving ventures.

Meet Eric Boisjoli:

Eric Boisjoli, the co-founder of Bold Commerce and President of Kickbooster Inc., is at the helm of Erk's Werks Business Group. With a passion for innovation, a keen eye for business opportunities, and an unwavering commitment to success, Eric brings a wealth of expertise and experience to our conglomerate.

Throughout his entrepreneurial journey, Eric has played a pivotal role in establishing and growing successful businesses. His leadership has propelled Bold Commerce into a global leader in eCommerce solutions, while Kickbooster Inc. became a driving force in the crowdfunding and affiliate marketing industry.

A Vision for the Future:

With a focus on profitability, exceptional customer experiences, and community revitalization, we envision a conglomerate that transcends industry boundaries and fosters a culture of growth and impact.

We understand the importance of empowering like-minded General Managers (GMs) and firmly believe in their potential to drive the success of acquired businesses. Together with the GMs, we foster a culture of collaboration, mentorship, and innovation, ensuring each business thrives under their guidance.

Creating a Legacy Together:

At Erk's Werks, we are more than just a collection of businesses. We are a family of changemakers, with Eric as our guiding force. Together, we build a legacy of sustainable success, driving growth, and making a positive impact on communities.
